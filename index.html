A coder with a knack for design

Hello there! I am junior at the University of Washington pursuing a degree in Informatics with a concentration in either Data Science or Human-Computer Interaction. I taught myself how to code HTML and CSS when I was only nine years old, and have since recreationally created several webpages. I never thought that my interest in coding would go beyond web design; however in college, I decided to take a Computer Science course for fun.

I quickly realized that I have a talent for development and went from taking business prerequisites to Informatics prerequisites. I was admitted into the competitive Informatics and HCDE programs this past summer, and am now a Teaching Assistant within the iSchool and a Web Development intern at Her Campus.
